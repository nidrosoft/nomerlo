# 1. OVERVIEW & USER JOURNEY

## 1.1 Tenant Portal Purpose

The Tenant Portal provides renters with a centralized platform to manage all aspects of their rental experience:
- Pay rent and view payment history
- Submit and track maintenance requests
- Access lease documents and property information
- Communicate with landlord/property manager
- Receive important notices and announcements
- Manage profile and notification preferences
